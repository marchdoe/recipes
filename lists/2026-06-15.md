## Notes

- **Tuesday pan-sear mod:** Do NOT buy vegetable/canola frying oil, flour, or beer — the recipe is being made with pan-seared fish instead of deep-fried battered fish. Just season mahi mahi with chili powder and sear in olive oil.
- **Wednesday street corn mod:** Greek yogurt replaces low-fat mayo for heart health — only buy mayo if you prefer the original recipe.
- **Chipotle can is dual-use:** The same can covers Tue taco salsa (1–2 whole peppers) and Sat chili (1 tbsp chopped). Buy one 7 oz can; refrigerate leftovers.
- **Cilantro quantity:** The four recipes collectively call for nearly 2 cups of cilantro. Buy 2 large bunches or 1 very large one.
- **Avocado ripeness:** Buy 3 — 1 that's ripe for Tue (or let it ripen by then), 1 slightly firm for Tue taco sauce (½ avocado), 1 for Sat chili topping.
- **Thai basil vs cilantro (Thu):** Thai basil gives the more authentic flavor for the noodle dish. If available at your store, buy a small bunch and use cilantro from the big bunch elsewhere.
- **Leftover plan:** Mon soup (6 servings) → Tue + Wed lunches; Sat chili (6 servings) → Sun dinner + Mon/Tue lunches next week (freeze extra portions).
