## Notes

- **Baja fish tacos — pan-sear only:** skip flour, beer, and frying oil entirely. Season fish with chili powder, pan-sear in a thin film of olive oil 3–4 min per side.
- **Chipotle peppers in adobo:** one can covers both Tuesday (salsa) and Saturday (chili). Freeze leftover chipotles in a small bag after opening.
- **Pesto soup broth:** bone broth adds richer flavor and a small collagen boost; regular low-sodium chicken broth works fine.
- **Thai basil:** if unavailable, regular basil or extra cilantro works in the noodles — flavor shifts slightly but the dish still works.
- **Wednesday mods:** replace the ¼ cup low-fat mayo AND ½ cup sour cream with plain non-fat Greek yogurt (already on the list). Skip the optional Parmesan garnish.
- **Saturday chili:** start with 1 tbsp butter and add more only if vegetables stick; the Ro-Tel tomatoes provide plenty of moisture. Use Greek yogurt instead of sour cream at the table.
- **Leftover plan:** Mon soup (6 servings) → Tue + Wed lunches. Sat chili (6 servings) → Sun dinner + Mon/Tue lunches next week. Freeze any chili beyond that.
