## Ingredients

### Roasted sweet potatoes
- 12 oz diced sweet potatoes (1 medium)
- 2½ tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp minced thyme
- 1 tsp salt
- ½ tsp black pepper

### Taco seasoning
- 1½ tsp garlic powder
- 1½ tsp cumin
- 1 tsp chili powder
- ½ tsp onion powder
- ½ tsp smoked paprika
- ½ tsp salt

### Taco meat
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 minced shallot
- 8 oz lean ground beef
- 2 minced garlic cloves
- 1½ tbsp tomato paste
- 3 tbsp water

### Assembly
- 8 oz cottage cheese
- ½ avocado, thinly sliced
- 2 tbsp pepitas
- 2 tsp hemp hearts
- 2 tbsp hot honey
- 1 tbsp minced cilantro

## Directions

### Sweet potatoes
1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Toss sweet potatoes with olive oil, thyme, salt, and pepper.
2. Spread on a baking sheet in a single layer.
3. Roast 22–28 minutes, tossing every 8–10 minutes until tender. Cool.

### Taco seasoning and meat
4. Mix all taco seasoning ingredients in a bowl.
5. Heat skillet over medium heat. Add olive oil and shallot; sauté 2–3 minutes until caramelized.
6. Add ground beef and garlic; break up and crumble evenly.
7. Sprinkle seasoning over beef; stir 1 minute until browned.
8. Add tomato paste and water; stir. Season with salt and pepper. Lower heat to medium-low; cook 1–2 minutes until thickened. Remove from heat.

### Assembly
9. Place ¼ of the ground beef in each bowl; divide sweet potatoes between bowls.
10. Top sweet potatoes with the remaining beef mixture.
11. Scoop cottage cheese into each bowl; top with avocado slices.
12. Top with pepitas and hemp hearts.
13. Finish with hot honey drizzle and cilantro.
