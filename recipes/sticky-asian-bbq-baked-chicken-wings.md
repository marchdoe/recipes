## Ingredients

### Chicken and coating
- 1 pound boneless chicken tenderloins or breasts (cut into nugget-sized pieces)
- ¾ cup buttermilk
- 1½ cups Panko or crushed corn flakes
- 2 tablespoons whole wheat flour or cornmeal
- Salt and pepper, to taste
- Non-stick spray or olive oil

### Sauce
- ⅓ cup hoisin sauce
- ½ cup low-sodium soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
- 1 teaspoon sambal oelek chili paste
- 1 teaspoon toasted sesame oil
- ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ¼ teaspoon ground cloves
- ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
- ¼ teaspoon black pepper

### Garnish
- 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
- 4 green onions, chopped

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 475°F. Line baking sheet with foil; spray with olive oil.
2. Place chicken in large bowl and pour buttermilk over it; mix well.
3. Combine Panko, flour, salt, and pepper in shallow dish.
4. Remove each chicken piece from buttermilk, dredge through crumb mixture, pressing gently. Arrange on prepared baking sheet without crowding. Spray with non-stick spray or oil.
5. Bake 10–12 minutes, flip gently using tongs, spray other side, and bake 10–12 minutes more. Remove from oven and reduce temperature to 400°F.
6. While chicken bakes, prepare sauce: whisk together hoisin, soy sauce, honey, ginger, sambal oelek, sesame oil, cinnamon, cloves, garlic powder, and pepper until smooth. Stir in 1 tablespoon sesame seeds.
7. Dip each chicken piece in sauce, draining excess, then return to pan. Repeat until all pieces are coated.
8. Place pan in 400°F oven for 5 minutes to warm through and set glaze.
9. Serve topped with green onions, remaining sesame seeds, and extra sauce for dipping.

## Notes

- **Sodium caution:** ½ cup soy sauce + ⅓ cup hoisin (also salty) drives sodium high. Use low-sodium soy as written; consider halving the hoisin or using less sauce per piece.
- **Heart-healthy notes:** chicken breast (lean) + oven-baked (no frying) + buttermilk dredge are good. Sweetness comes from honey, not refined sugar.
- **Dinner pairing per source:** rice + roasted broccoli for a complete meal.
- **Air fryer option** available — cook in batches to crisp; internal temp 165°F.
- **Make-ahead:** reheat at 350°F for 10–15 min.
