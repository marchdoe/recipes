## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 2 tablespoons salted butter
- 1 yellow onion, chopped
- 2 shallots, sliced
- 6 cloves garlic, chopped
- 4 ribs celery, chopped
- 6 carrots, chopped
- 2 tablespoons fresh thyme leaves
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh rosemary
- 1 teaspoon crushed fennel
- Salt and black pepper, to taste
- 8 cups chicken bone broth
- 1 pound boneless chicken breasts or thighs
- 2 cups chopped broccoli florets
- ½ cup basil pesto
- 2 cups baby spinach, chopped
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 2–3 cups egg noodles
- ¼ cup chopped parsley
- Parmesan and fresh dill for serving

## Directions

### Stovetop method

1. Melt olive oil and butter in a large Dutch oven over medium heat. Add onions and cook 3–5 minutes. Add shallots, garlic, celery, carrots, thyme, rosemary, and fennel. Season with salt and pepper; cook 5 minutes more.
2. Pour in broth and bring to a boil. Stir in chicken and broccoli (optionally add a parmesan rind). Cover and cook 20 minutes until chicken is cooked through.
3. Shred chicken and remove rind. Stir in pesto, spinach, and lemon juice. Taste and adjust seasoning. Return to boil, add noodles, cook 6–8 minutes until tender. Stir in parsley.
4. Serve topped with parmesan and dill.

### Crockpot method

1. Combine oil, butter, onions, shallots, garlic, celery, carrots, thyme, rosemary, and fennel in crockpot.
2. Add broth, chicken, broccoli, and optional parmesan rind. Season with salt and pepper. Cook 3–4 hours on high or 6–8 hours on low.
3. Switch to high heat. Shred chicken, remove rind. Stir in pesto, spinach, and lemon. Add noodles and cook 10–15 minutes until soft. Add parsley.
4. Serve with parmesan and dill.

## Notes

- **Heart-health note:** butter + pesto + parmesan + egg noodles add saturated fat and refined carbs. Mitigations:
  - Use 1 tbsp butter (or all olive oil)
  - Halve the pesto, or use a lower-oil/dairy-free pesto
  - Sub whole-grain noodles or add more vegetables in place of noodles
- **Big batch:** 6 servings — covers two adults for dinner + 2 lunches.
