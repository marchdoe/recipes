## Ingredients

- 12 ounces salt pork, sliced
- 1½ cups milk or buttermilk
- 2 teaspoons ground black pepper
- ½ cup all-purpose flour
- Peanut oil for frying (¼-inch deep)

## Directions

1. Place sliced salt pork in a container and cover with milk or buttermilk. Let sit for several hours to draw out salt. Remove pork from milk and discard milk.
2. Pepper each slice of salt pork and dredge lightly in flour.
3. Add peanut oil to ¼-inch depth in a heavy skillet. Heat over medium-high heat. Carefully add prepared salt pork slices. Cook, turning once, until lightly browned and cooked through (about 7–8 minutes total).
4. Remove to a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.
